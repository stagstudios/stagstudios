FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly do I get for $600? +
A fully custom 3–5 page website (Home, About, Services, Contact, and an optional Gallery), plus Google Business Profile setup, Apple Maps listing, and Yelp profile creation. We also include 30 days of minor text and image tweaks, domain setup, hosting configuration, and a printed management brochure so you can maintain your site independently.
Are there any monthly fees? +
No recurring fees from us. The only ongoing cost is your domain renewal (typically $10–$15/year) and hosting if applicable. We set everything up so you're in full control — no strings attached.
How long does it take to build? +
Most sites go live within 7 to 10 days from our initial call. We'll send you a preview to approve before anything goes public, and we handle all the technical publishing.
What if I need changes after the 30 days? +
Content updates are $50–$75 per batch. A "batch" means a set of related changes — like updating your hours, swapping a few images, or adding a seasonal promotion. We define this clearly in writing before you commit.
Do I own the website? +
Yes — 100%. Once we hand it off, the code, the domain, and all the content belongs to you. We provide a management brochure so you or anyone else can maintain it. We're completely hands-off after the sale.
Can you build e-commerce or online ordering? +
Our core package focuses on brochure-style sites — designed to showcase your business, build trust, and drive foot traffic. We don't do complex e-commerce or payment systems, but what we build is specifically engineered to make people visit your location.
